Hexbound Realms (Alpha)

Starter Guide

Units: Train units using gold. Some units require tech unlocks. Units gain XP and can be upgraded.

Buildings: Construct in cities to boost income, defense, or unlock capabilities.

Tech Tree: Research techs to unlock stronger units and abilities.

Quests: Complete quests to gain rewards such as gold, units, or tech boosts.

Victory: Win by defeating all enemies or completing campaign objectives.
